About WordPress

WordPress (WP, or WordPress. org) is an internet content monitoring system. It was initially produced as a device to publish blog sites yet has actually developed to sustain releasing various other web material, including more traditional web sites, newsletter and Internet forum, media galleries, membership websites, learning monitoring systems, and online shops. Readily available as totally free and open-source software, WordPress is amongst one of the most popular web content administration systems –-- it was made use of by 43. 1% of the top 10 million internet sites since December 2023. WordPress is created in the PHP language and coupled with a MySQL or MariaDB data source. Attributes include a plugin style and a theme system, referred to within WordPress as "Motifs". To operate, WordPress has to be installed on an internet server, either as part of an Internet hosting service or on a computer running the WordPress software. WordPress was released on May 27, 2003, by its creators, American designer Matt Mullenweg and English developer Mike Little.
